Everything you need to know about training at Rack City.

You must use headphones when listening to audio. Please be respectful of other members training nearby.

Yes. Every location has a designated changing area where you can change privately. Take your time — no rush before or after your session.

We currently do not have showers at our facilities. Given the automated nature of our locations, we've opted not to add the complexity of unsupervised shower facilities — for now.

If you go more than 5 minutes past your reserved time, the system will automatically charge you a fee for the additional time. Please make sure your rack is cleared when your session ends. You're welcome to stay in the facility to collect your belongings, but your rack must be cleared within 5 minutes. If the next slot is available, you can extend your session directly through the app.

You can cancel or reschedule any booking directly through the app — no calls, no emails. Cancel at least 2 hours before your session and there is no charge. Cancellations within 2 hours are subject to a fee, because we likely won't be able to rebook that slot on short notice. Please cancel if you can't make it — we appreciate the courtesy.

The minimum booking time is 30 minutes. Additional time can be booked in half-hour increments.

Peak hours are 5–9pm on weekdays (Monday–Friday). Off-peak is all other times — early mornings, midday, late nights, and all day on weekends.

Pay-As-You-Go: A great option for anyone who wants to train without a commitment. There's no pressure to become a member — Pay-As-You-Go works perfectly as your regular gym. Note that memberships are capped, so Pay-As-You-Go will always be available even when memberships are full. If you find yourself coming more than once or twice a week, a membership will save you money — but it's entirely up to you.

RackCity+: Includes 10 hours of off-peak rack time per month — ideal for lifters who train regularly during off-peak hours (all times except 5–9pm weekdays).

RackCity Unlimited: Includes 1 hour per day at any time slot, including peak hours — ideal if you want maximum flexibility to train any time of day.
